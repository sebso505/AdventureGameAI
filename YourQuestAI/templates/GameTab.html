You are Johnny and you are entering the world of Harry Potter. You find yourself in the midst of a wizarding world full of mystery, wonders and adventure. You have been chosen to pursue a quest: to find the Philosopher's Stone and protect the world against dark forces. You must explore the magical world and uncover the secrets of the Stone before it falls into the wrong hands. As you venture forth and discover the various mysteries, you are accompanied by an owl, who serves as your guide. The stakes are high and the dangers great, but you must face them all and prove yourself worthy of the challenge. After all, the fate of the world is in your hands. What is your choice, Johnny?
